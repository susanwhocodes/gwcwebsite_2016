Women's empowerment refers to increasing and improving the social, economic, political and legal strength of the women, to ensure equal-right to women, and to make them confident enough to claim their rights
About
What is Girls Who Code?
Girls Who Code is a group that inspires, educates, and equips girls with the computing skills to pursue 21st century opportunities.
This website is the final project for the Andover branch of Girls Who Code where the theme is women's empowerment.

Half Sunrise
A choose your own adventure game featuring a young woman facing the pressures and stereotypes of society.

Instructions: Use the arrow keys to move your character around the screen. Press the spacebar to defeat the monster and move to the next screen.
Life Uncertain
Jump into the exciting world of either Abigail Kenzi Or Tabatha Thompson. Help them live their life and fulfill their dreams without their gender getting in the way. Go to school, get a job, marry, and overcome problems.
Will you help them build up enough courage to stand up for their rights?

Instructions: Enter your answers in all lowercase letters. Then hit OK. Your answers will determine the end result of your game, and change the experience.

Who Killed Equal Pay?
Ah good, you are just in time for the game. Someone has committed a heinous crime…. Killing Equal Pay! All you have to do, is find why the wage gap between genders exists! Adventure around the house, and find clues scattered in different rooms to deduce one of the 6 main reasons behind this.
Instructions: Click to roll. Click to move, must click on doors to enter room.
Amazonia
This game is about an Amazon princess trying to dodge bullets coming at her. The bullets have different insults directed to girls on them, and the princess wants to avoid the insults so her self esteem doesn’t sink lower. The goal of this game is to last as long as possible without losing all three lives.

Instructions: Move the mouse up and down to move the Amazon princess and dodge the bullets. If you hit a bullet, you will lose a life. Try to dodge as many bullets as you can without losing all three lives. Hit space to pause, and the restart button on the bottom to restart.
